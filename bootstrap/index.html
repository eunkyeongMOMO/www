WATCH THE SHOW
SAINT LAURENT - WOMEN'S WINTER 22 SHOW

Set at the base of the Eiffel Tower, Saint Laurent proves runway shows are back and bigger than ever before. The brand’s Spring/Summer 2022 show staged an epically large runway circling a wall of lights with Paris’ iconic monument in the background as the platform for 62 new 80’s-inspired looks for the Parisian fashion house.

MAGAZIME

  • Rosé and Saint Laurent’s Love Affair

    To honor such an important figure in the shaping of Saint Laurent’s style, an exclusive list of VIPs graced the front row of the show. Among them, Saint Laurent’s current darling, BLACKPINK’s Rosé, surely stole the spotlight.

  • The Only Way Is Forward

    The relationship between Rosé and the French fashion house, Saint Laurent, has come a long way. From the star’s first invitation to Paris Fashion Week to making history at the MET Gala in a Saint Laurent dress, the partnership has become one of the most successful team-ups in the world of fashion.

Yves Saint Laurent

“Chanel freed women, and I empowered them.”

Now a fixture of the Parisian jetset. Saint Laurent reached dizzying heights, with a roster of famous clients clamoring over his couture and pret-a-porter collections. Introducing sheer blouses and jumpsuits to women’s wardrobes, the designer became an icon.

The stereotype of fashion is that it's flighty. Trends may come and go and return again several years or decades later. But in the history of fashion, there are unforgettable figures who remain influential and their designs, eternal. And a huge swath of that past belongs to arguably one of the greatest couturiers who ever lived, Yves Saint Laurent.
